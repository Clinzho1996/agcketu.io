ABOUT AGC NIGERIA

The Assemblies of God, Nigeria, has a population of 3.6 million adherents and members worshipping in over 16,300 churches in Nigeria. The General Council with its Headquarters in Enugu in Enugu State of Nigeria provides ministerial credentials to more than 15,650 ministers, including more than 50 missionaries in over 9 countries and territories of the world.

Rev. Dr. Ejikeme Ejim is the current General Superintendent of the Assemblies of God, Nigeria. He was elected to this position at the 36th General Council in October 2019. Until elected in 2019 to this office, he was the Assistant General Superintendent . Previously He served as District Superintendent of Lagos Mainland District of referred to as “Jerusalem” District.

He has also pioneered International Prayer Initiative for over twenty years. A renowned international speaker, he has served in various capacities in Umuahia District and national level. He is a member of the Executive Committee. Reverend Dr. Okoroafor is happily married to Rev. Mrs. Stella Okoroafor. Their marriage is blessed with five lovely children to the glory of God.
